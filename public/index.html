Information

G.O.D. is conveniently located in the "Heart of the Uwharrie Mountains”, Eldorado, North Carolina. It’s just 5 minutes from the Eldorado Outpost where country food, camping and outdoor equipment abound, 10 minutes North of Troy, and 30 minutes South of Asheboro. Training and OHV Trail Rides leave from the Uwharrie Off-Road Training Center (4026 NC-109, Troy, NC 27371). Other nearby attractions are the Uwharrie Nat’l Forest, N.C. Zoo, Badin Lake, Seagrove Pottery, Richard Petty Museum, N.C. Aviation Museum, and many more to fill your weekend.
