4x4 Trail Rides

Miles of OHV trails to enjoy!

Embark on an exciting trail ride. Miles of backcountry trails in the rolling hills provide beginners as well as seasoned drivers beautiful terrain to traverse. You’ll encounter hills, ruts, creek crossings, steep inclines, holes, ditched trails, bog holes, off-camber and rock. We’ll design your trail ride for the skill level of your drivers.

Canoeing & Kayaking

Paradise is just a paddle away.

The rivers flow not past, but through us, thrilling, tingling, vibrating every fiber and cell of the substance of our bodies, making them glide and sing. -- John Muir
The Uwharrie river provides a beautiful float from Low Water Bridge to the Highway 109 takeout. Ledger Shoals is in the middle of this pristine stretch, but be sure to check the water levels as it's a free-flowing river that changes often.

Catered Uwharrie

A 5 star meal on our majestic multi-purpose picnic area that overlooks Ledger Shoals of Uwharrie.

The local Eldorado Outpost Caterers serve G.O.D. guests with delectable grilled gourmet or southern foods served on location ‘down by the river’. Their marinated grilled chicken is out of this world. Enjoying food that is prepared to perfection while dining at the ‘Grand Blue Slate Stone Tables’ and watching the river flow by is an unforgettable experience.

Outdoor Encounters

Experiential education that "sticks".

Consider an environmental learning adventure for your club, group or Scouts. Sampling the river for aquatic species, fishing, collecting and examining tiny water creatures, gold panning in the shallows, all are great fun. Possibilities for environmental stewardship and outdoor learning are endless. Bring your own instructor, or we have two retired Park Rangers with a wealth of knowledge just waiting to engage your students with an outdoor skills session or natural resource education.

Primitive Camping

Outside is the New Inside! Grab your Gear and GO!

Camp under the stars or right by the river. Primitive means you bring everything in, take everything out. You have wide open spaces with no neighbors, no noise, no outside influence. Kids can be kids and mom and dad can be kids too. Where else can you backcountry camp without hours of hiking into the woods or right beside another tent pad? Drive your 4x4 right to your site.
